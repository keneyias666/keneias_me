About Me

I'm a 4th year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student with a slightly deep love for the intersection of technology and visual communication — a quiet little polymath in the making, if you will.

When I'm not building systems or debugging code (yes, the coffee-fueled kind), you'll find me obsessing over kerning like it's a personality trait, crafting type-led posters with meticulous care, or getting lost in the aesthetics of editorial design. Typography and Graphic Art isn't just a hobby of mine — it's a lens through which I see all things.

I'm a firm believer that progress, not perfection is the name of the game. I believe great software and great design are the same thing — intentional, refined, and purposeful. There's a certain magic in making the functional feel beautiful.

Currently open to opportunities where I can code with care and design with heart. Let's make something together.
